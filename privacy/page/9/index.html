Last updated: 1 October 2022

We do not want to collect or store any of your personal data. If you notice anything on this website that doesn’t follow this principle, please drop us an email. With that in mind, we want to share the following information.

Vamsi Myla

I am a Graduate Student at Portland State University in the Department of Computer Science. I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at Portland State University.

My research interest lies in designing scalable and privacy-preserving data management systems that integrate fine-grained access control with real-world privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA. I am particularly interested in exploring middleware techniques, query rewriting, and policy-aware optimization strategies to enhance secure and efficient data access in large-scale environments.
